Experiment X - Subject/Topic

Description

An ancient dungeon in the desert with blue and purple walls, the dungeon is randomly filled with shelters (houses) and the treasure chests.

Technical

"-" represents the dirt in the background, "." represents the dungeon rooms and hallways. Wall tiles are automatically placed around the ".". The "1" in the dungeon represents a shelter (house) and the "2" represents a treasure chest.
